Location

ADCS 2011 will be held at the Australian National University, Canberra. We will be in the Haydon-Allen Lecture Theatre ("the tank"), which is in the courtyard of the Haydon-Allen Building. The building is just off University Ave.

ALTW 2011 will be next door in Haydon-Allen G040.

There are a number of cafes, restaurants and pubs within five minutes walk. The city centre is 10-15 minutes walk.
